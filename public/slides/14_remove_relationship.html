Remove Forked Relationship

Settings/General
Go to Settings/General.
Settings/General/Advanced
Remove Forked Relationship
Under General, find 'Advanced' and expand to see all options.

Once expanded, find the 'Remove Forked Relationship' option.
Confirmation Modal
A modal will pop up to confirm the removal. You must type in the name of the project to complete the action. This action prevents users from accidentally removing a forked relationship.
Success!
Once complete, you will see an alert telling you that the forked relationship has been removed.
